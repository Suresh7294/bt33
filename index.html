Global Chesss Academy


With a legacy spanning over 50 years, our Chess Academy has been a trusted name in nurturing minds and building champions. Founded on the principles of discipline, strategy, and continuous learning, we offer structured training programs for all levels—from beginners taking their first move to advanced players aiming for titles. Our experienced coaches, many of whom are certified and titled players, provide personalized guidance through modern and effective teaching methods. We host regular tournaments, practice sessions, and performance evaluations, ensuring each student not only learns but thrives. Join us and become part of a legacy where excellence in chess is a tradition.
